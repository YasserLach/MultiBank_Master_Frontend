Atlas Bank

The Bank of Africa, formerly known as BMCE Bank of Africa or Moroccan Bank for Foreign Trade, is a Moroccan commercial bank. Founded in 1959 as a public bank, it was privatized in 1995 and later acquired by the financier Othman Benjelloun through the RMA insurance company, which he controls.
